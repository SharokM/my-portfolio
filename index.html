A little bit about me.

My love for tech started during my decade long career in technical (full stack & devops) talent acquisition. I loved leading teams of sourcers, working closely with scrum teams, assembling the right talent for complex integration projects, and organizing key note speakers to motivate and inspire the engineers. However, the more buzzwords I heard, the more curious I became. I desired to understand my candidates on a more technical level to ensure I was the best at my job. That's when I discovered and fell in love with coding & web design!

As a front-end developer, I pride myself on writing clean, efficient code that's easy to read and build upon. My goal with every site is to provide a streamlined user experience whilst displaying my skills and allowing for scalability. Besides web development, I enjoy gardening, reading and travelling!
