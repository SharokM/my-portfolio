A little bit about me.

After a decade long career in technical talent acquisition, I decided to learn the secrets behind the buzzwords. However, the more buzzwords I heard, the more curious I became. I desired to understand my candidates on a more technical level to ensure I was the best at my job. That's when I discovered and fell in love with coding & web design!

As a front-end developer, I pride myself on writing clean, efficient code that's easy to read and build upon. My goal with every site is to provide a streamlined user experience whilst displaying my skills and allowing for scalability. Besides web development, I enjoy gardening, reading and travelling!
